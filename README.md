# 📊 Business Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Business Performance Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.
The dashboard analyzes sales data to help businesses understand their **revenue trends, profitability, customer performance, and regional sales distribution**.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into **meaningful business insights** that support data-driven decision making.

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall **business revenue and profit performance**
* Identify **sales trends over time**
* Understand **customer and product contribution**
* Evaluate **sales channels and income groups**
* Identify **top-performing products and customers**

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ Summary Page

The summary page provides key business metrics:

* **Total Revenue:** $1.24 Billion
* **Total Profit:** $461.77 Million
* **Total Orders:** 64K
* **Profit Margin:** 37.36%
* **Average Order Value:** $19.28K

These KPIs help quickly evaluate the **overall business health**.

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Trends Analysis

This section analyzes revenue patterns and growth:

* Revenue trend by **year and month**
* **Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth**
* Revenue distribution by **sales channel**
* Revenue by **income group**
* Revenue by **region type**

This helps identify **growth patterns and market demand**.

---

## 3️⃣ Customer & Product Insights

### Top Customers by Revenue

Identifies the **highest revenue generating customers**, helping businesses focus on key accounts.

### Top Products by Revenue

Highlights the **best performing products**, useful for:

* Inventory planning
* Marketing strategy
* Product prioritization

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

* **Wholesale channel contributes the highest revenue share (~54%)**
* **Lower-middle income group generates the largest revenue (~45%)**
* **Cities generate significantly higher revenue compared to towns and villages**
* **A small group of products drives a large portion of revenue**

These insights help businesses focus on **high-value customers, products, and markets**.
